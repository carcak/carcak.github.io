Community Outreach and participation in Local Policy

Community Outreach

  • 2021 Carrion-Klier, C., Nieto, A., MAGMA group. Conversation in a local radio about women in science and in drone piloting. Radio Santa Cruz, Galápagos, Ecuador.
  • 2018, 2019, 2021 Carrion-Klier, C., CDF. Vegetation Mapping with Drones. Presentations and activities in the Charles Darwin Foundation (CDF) open house, for up to 2000 local community visitors. CDF, Santa Cruz, Galápagos, Ecuador.
  • 2020 Rodriguez, J., Carrion-Klier, C., CDF ECO-Program. Restoration of the Scalesia Forest. Presentation and learning activities for local high school students. Virtual webinar.
  • 2019 Carrion-Klier, C.. Invasive Plant Species in Galápagos Local radio interview in Radio Antena 9. Santa Cruz, Galápagos, Ecuador.
  • 2019 Carrion-Klier, C., CDF ECO-Program. Learning to navigate with map and compass. Three day practical workshop for local mid-school students at Tomás de Berlanga school, a National Geographic supported school, on the use of maps and development of visual-spatial intelligence. Santa Cruz, Galápagos, Ecuador.
  • 2019 Jäger, H., Carrion-Klier, C.. Mapping of Invasive Species. Informational panel for visitors of CDF's exhibit hall. CDF, Santa Cruz, Galápagos, Ecuador.
  • 2018 Carrion-Klier, C.. The Scientific Method. Presentation on the use of the scientific method to local high school students. Tomás de Berlanga School, Santa Cruz, Galápagos, Ecuador.
